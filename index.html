Hello, this is web designer Sujeong Ha.
In this rapidly changing era, users are looking for more convenient and simple things
in the countless service platforms, and this has had a significant impact on the visual perspective as well.

My goal is to create a user-centered design.
I think my job is to provide a great experience to users and increase the value of products or brands through a service platform.
I am very interested in observing and analyzing to build user-centered design, and
I am constantly studying how to use screens using CSS and Javascript for collaboration and maintenance.

I will become a person who can see, hear, experience, and create customized designs that were obtained through vivid delivery .
